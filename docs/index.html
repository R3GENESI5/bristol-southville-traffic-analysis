Key Finding

The survey data does not contain evidence sufficient to demonstrate that vehicles are using Southville's residential streets as a cut-through between Coronation Road and North Street. The ANPR camera network had no cameras placed within the Southville zone. The two nearest cameras recorded only 10-11 vehicles per day appearing at both locations, representing approximately 0.07-0.14% of the traffic at those cameras.
